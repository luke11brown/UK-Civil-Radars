Yup they are radar derived images - the wavelength of the radar is designed to pick up the precipitation.

Couple of things to bear in mind when looking at weather radar data -

1. Not all precipitation can be seen on the radar - IIRC snow and light drizzle isn't
2. Beyond the Radar's range there is no depiction of precipitation (logical of course) but that means that the screen looks blank which may lead to thinking there is no precipitation in that area.

No doubt there are more but I have only limited experience, certainly with in-flight kit.

I use meteox.nl for flight planning when the weather is interesting.
